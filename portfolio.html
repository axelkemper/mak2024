My First Project!


we made a burger maker in which it customizes a burger to one's liking! The program asks if you want cheese, lettuce, and sauce. Depending on if you type yes or no, it will add each component of the burger.


Rat maze


The player uses the arrow keys to move the mouse through the maze while collecting as many pieces of cheese as possible. The cheese respawns to different locations in the maze and the objective is to collect the cheese while escaping with a 40 second time limit.


Scratch project


We made a story where our character leaves his house for a couple hours to go on an adventure. He travels several environments on his horse and finds a magic wand to take with him. As he is on his journey, he encounters a bear in which he fights and uses his magic wand to turn him into a harmless bunny. He then travels back home and rests up for the next day
